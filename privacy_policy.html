Privacy Policy for Joozdlog

Effective Date:11-feb-2024

Introduction

Joozdlog is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your personal and sensitive information in accordance with Google Play's User Data policy. It applies to all users of the Joozdlog app and visitors to our website.

Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at:

  • Email: joozdlog@joozd.nl

Data Collection and Use

Joozdlog is designed to prioritize user privacy:

  • Local Storage: All flight data inputted into the Joozdlog app is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor is it stored on our servers.
  • Email for Backup: Users can opt to provide an email address for receiving backup files. This email is not stored directly; instead, we keep a securely encrypted reference to it. This ensures backups are sent to the correct user while maintaining privacy.
  • Feedback and Contact Data: If you use our feedback feature, you may choose to provide contact information. This data is temporarily stored on our servers and used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiries or feedback.

Third-Party Services

- Google ML Kit: The app uses Google ML Kit for features involving the camera, such as scanning text or QR codes. Google may collect usage statistics and other data. For details, please review the Google ML Kit Terms of Service at Google ML Kit Terms.

Data Sharing

Joozdlog does not share your personal or sensitive data with third parties, except as described above with Google ML Kit or as required by law.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you choose to provide an email for backup purposes, the reference to this email is kept securely and is deleted upon your request or if you cease using our backup service.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to our information practices. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email (sent to the email address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on this website prior to the change becoming effective.

Consent and Acceptance

By using Joozdlog, you consent to the data practices described in this privacy policy.
